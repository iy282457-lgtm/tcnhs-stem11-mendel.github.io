Mendel Celebrations

Hugyaw Pasundayag: Luck Came Short (9/29/24)

Hugyaw Pasundayag is a yearly event that took place at STI College Gymnasium. This prosperous event was exclusively designed for Senior High School students and was divided into four clusters, namely: "Air Nomads", Fire Nation" ,"Earth Kingdom", and "Water Tribe". Section Mendel was assigned to the Air Nomads, along with other sections, including Euler, Copernicus, and Tesla. Each clusters took it's time preparing for the event, working together, and doing their best to showcase their creativity and hard work. The cluster Air Nomads where section Mendel was assigned, finished off with a 2nd place, being the Earth Kingdom victorious.

Foundation Day: The Triumphant Gryffindors (2/14/25)

Foundation Day is a yearly event that took place at TCNHS Gymnasium celebrating the school's 58th year anniversary, with each year featuring a unique and distinctive theme. This year introduced an exciting theme "Alohomora" which included clusters named "Gryffindor", "Slytherin", "Hufflepuff", and "Ravenclaw". Section Mendel was assigned to the House of Gryffindors along with the other sections from Senior High School and Junior High School. Each clusters took their time to prepare for their yell-making, dramas, and lip-singing. The event ended successfully reigning the House of Gryffindors triumphant, bringing joyful and memorable experiences not only to the Mendelians but also to the students involved in this event.

Research Defense: Efforts Paid Off (2/20/25)

Research defense is a formal event where each group of researchers present and discuss their research findings, methodology, and conclusions to a panel of academic experts. This brought Mendelians to numerous struggles from finding a topic to creating a research paper to be presented in front of the experts. The groups made their preparations before the defense, taking the most important and necessary information about their research. The Mendelians went through numerous struggles but in the end, their hard work paid off.

Hope Day: The Victorious Mendelians (3/7/25)

Hope Day is a yearly event that took place at Section Mendel. This event is only exclusive to Senior High School students. The purpose of this event is to create a game for Grade 12 students to enjoy and served as an 3rd quarterly exam for the Mendelians. Each section is required to create a game, and their goal is to provide excitement and satisfaction to students. The test scores of the Mendelians rely on the feedback that students give. This encouraged them to work as a team and try their best to provide students the best experience. At the end of the event, the Mendelians went triumphant bringing a trophy and pride to their section.

MMCM: The Victory (3/2/25)

MMCM event was an event that took place at Davao City. The Mendelians joined the event to bring triumph and pride to their sections. They took their time into practicing their moves for the upcoming event despite having difficulties in balancing their academic lives. At the end of their performance, they reigned victorious and brought pride amongst the Mendelians.

The Strong Scouts of Section Mendel (10/7/24)

Rover Scouts are the pride of the school. Mendelians, not wanting to be left out, volunteered to join to bring pride to both the school and the section. They spent a night outdoors despite facing issues along the way. This showed how brave the students of Section Mendel and why they deserve to be recognized as a role model.

The Stargazers (3/21/25)

Mendelians are also interested in stars. Their curiosity led them to witness the beauty of the cosmos. It served as an unforgettable experience for the Mendelians to enjoy.
